Our Story

Delaware Golf Carts was founded with a simple mission: to provide the finest electric vehicles to our community while delivering unparalleled customer service and support.

As an authorized dealer for both DENAGO and EVOLUTION, we bring together two of the industry's most respected brands under one roof, offering our customers the widest selection of premium electric vehicles available.

Our commitment to excellence extends beyond sales to include comprehensive service, maintenance, and customer support that keeps your vehicle running at peak performance.
